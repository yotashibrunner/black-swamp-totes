FAQ

Common questions

How many bins do I need?

As a guide — studio or dorm: 20 bins. 1–2 bedroom: 35 bins. 3–4 bedroom: 50 bins. When in doubt, go one size up. Extensions are easy if you need more time.

When do you deliver and pick up?

You choose your delivery date and pickup date when booking. We coordinate a time window that works for you.

What if I need more time?

No problem. Extensions are automatically billed at $0.30 per bin per day. You can schedule a later pickup from your booking page.

Are the bins clean?

Every bin is sanitized before delivery. We spray and wipe each bin between every rental.

What if I lose or damage a bin?

Lost or damaged bins are charged at $35 per bin to the card on file. We track each bin by number so we know exactly what comes back.

Do I need to be home for delivery and pickup?

We prefer it, but if you need a contactless drop or pickup just let us know in the booking notes and we will coordinate.
